New York, NY

New York is the most populous city in the United States. The historical For-Sale Inventory data shows that New York real estate market had a seasonality, which the supply in January is usually the lowest and July is usually the highest. However, in May 2020, the for-sale inventory hits the lowest point since January 2018 which might be due to COVID-19 situation. The Simple Exponential Smoothing model predicts that the supply of for-sale inventory will slightly decline and sale price will drop with around 35k in average in couple months.
